Classify an email as UNINTERESTING if it is any of the following:
- Spam or unsolicited email
- Marketing or promotional content
- University course updates sent to the entire class (e.g. assignment reminders, lecture notes, syllabus changes)
- Automated notifications or newsletters

Classify an email as INTERESTING if it is any of the following:
- A personal email written directly to me
- An email among friends or acquaintances
- Emails related to a job search, internship application, or career opportunity
- Any email with personal or emotional significance
